We have an idea for more useful and friendly medical organizer!
We introduce The Pill - web application that is easy to use tool for medical practice management. Current version consists:

  • Patient Register
    Availiable in Patients tab lets You add new patient profiles to patient register. Provides a quick overview in Your customers details. Gives You information about upcomming visits in easy and instant way.

  • Schedule
    Patient database integrated calendar. Absolutelly necessery for every medical practice. The perspectives availible - daily, weekly and monthly.

  • Services
    Before You arrange your first appointment, declare services for Your practice such as routine advice or tooth extraction. This soulution minimizes time You have to spend on appointment registration.
